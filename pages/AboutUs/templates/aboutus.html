About Us

Our company is made for connect between drivers and passengers so they can share together their mutual
ride to their destination.
We see a great importance in taking care of the environment by getting people to share their rides and also
providing passengers a way to rich their destination and to cheaper the drivers expenses.

FAQ

Are Animals allowed on the ride?
passengers are welcome aboard rides with their animal or pet according to the driver's approval
What is the price a passenger should pay to the driver?
The price range of the ride is determined by the driver.
Every driver who posts his ride need to add the price he is asked from one passenger.
How do I join a ride?
Clicking on the "looking for a ride" button in the home page will lead you to the results page.
In the result page you can look for the ride that is best for you and ask to join the ride.
After that you fill out a form with all the necessary details about the ride that are needed to be filled
and wait for the diver to approve it.
